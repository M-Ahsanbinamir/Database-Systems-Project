Northern Areas

  • Official Language: Urdu, English, and various regional languages (including Shina, Burushaski, Wakhi, and Balti)
  • Mountain Ranges: Karakoram Range, Himalayas, Hindu Kush Range
  • Rivers: Indus River, Gilgit River, Hunza River, Shigar River

Breathtaking mountains, glaciers, and stunning natural landscapes

The Northern Areas of Pakistan are a picturesque region renowned for their breathtaking mountain landscapes, including the Karakoram and Himalayan ranges, crystal-clear lakes, and vibrant cultures, making it a popular destination for adventurers and nature lovers.

City Destinations

Azad Jammu and Kashmir

  • Scenic mountainous region
  • Controlled by Pakistan government
  • Rich in natural beauty
  • Home to diverse communities

Heaven on Earth

It is a region in Pakistan-administered Kashmir with stunning natural beauty, encompassing lush valleys, cascading rivers, and majestic mountains. It holds significant cultural and historical importance, offering a tranquil escape and opportunities for outdoor activities like hiking and fishing.

Gilgit - Baltistan - Skardu

  • Majestic mountain landscapes
  • Home to K2 and Nanga Parbat
  • Rich cultural heritage
  • Adventure and trekking paradise

Land of Giants

Located in northern Pakistan, is a captivating region known for its mesmerizing landscapes, including towering peaks, pristine lakes, and ancient monasteries. It serves as a gateway to the world-famous Karakoram mountain range and offers thrilling mountaineering and trekking experiences.

Swat Valley

  • Scenic paradise with lush greenery
  • Rich historical and cultural heritage
  • Serene rivers and cascading waterfalls
  • Popular for outdoor adventure activities

Switzerland of the East

It is a scenic paradise renowned for its lush green meadows, crystal-clear rivers, and snow-capped peaks. It captivates visitors with its rich cultural heritage, archaeological sites, and opportunities for hiking, skiing, and exploring ancient Buddhist relics.

Hunza

  • Majestic mountains and stunning landscapes
  • Rich cultural heritage and traditions
  • Warm hospitality and friendly locals
  • Gateway to the Karakoram Highway

The Shangri-La of Pakistan

It is known for its majestic mountains, including Rakaposhi and Ultar peaks, and the tranquil beauty of the Hunza River. It enthralls visitors with its vibrant culture, welcoming locals, terraced fields, and apricot blossoms, offering a serene and enchanting experience.

Chitral Valley

  • Scenic beauty with towering peaks
  • Cultural diversity and traditional festivals
  • Home to Kalash tribe
  • Rich in unique flora and fauna

Land of Contrasts

It is a scenic gem known for its pristine beauty. The valley is home to stunning mountain ranges, including the Hindu Kush, and offers captivating attractions such as the Kalash Valley, Shandur Polo Festival, and a rich cultural heritage.

Turkey

  • Religion: Islam
  • Currency: Turkish Lira
  • Official Language: Turkish
  • Calling Code: +90

Historic charm, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes await in Turkey

Turkey, a captivating blend of East and West, offers ancient wonders, vibrant bazaars, breathtaking landscapes, and warm hospitality. Discover awe-inspiring historical sites like Istanbul's Hagia Sophia, savor delicious cuisine, explore stunning coastlines, and immerse yourself in a rich tapestry of culture and history.

City Destinations

Istanbul

  • Historic and vibrant city
  • East meets West
  • Breathtaking architecture and skyline
  • Rich cultural melting pot

City of Seven Hills

Istanbul, the transcontinental metropolis, bridges Europe and Asia, pulsating with history, culture, and beauty. Its majestic mosques, bustling bazaars, and the iconic Bosporus strait offer a captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern delights, making it an enchanting destination that leaves a lasting impression.

Ankara

  • Capital city of Turkey
  • Political and administrative center
  • Historic and cultural landmarks
  • Ankara Citadel, Atatürk Mausoleum

Capital city of Turkey

Ankara, the capital of Turkey, is a vibrant city blending modernity with history. It is known for its government institutions, universities, and cultural landmarks like Ataturk's Mausoleum. With a rich heritage and a bustling urban atmosphere, Ankara offers a unique blend of traditions and contemporary life.

Antalya

  • Coastal paradise with stunning beaches
  • Rich history and ancient ruins
  • Vibrant nightlife and entertainment
  • Beautiful landscapes and natural wonders

Turkish Riviera

Antalya is a picturesque coastal city in southwestern Turkey, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and ancient ruins. With its charming old town, beautiful marina, and a backdrop of the Taurus Mountains, Antalya offers a perfect blend of history, natural beauty, and modern amenities.

Thailand

  • Religion: Buddhism
  • Currency: Thai Bhat
  • Official Language: Thai
  • Calling Code: +66

Breathtaking beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality await in Thailand

Thailand, known as the "Land of Smiles," is a vibrant Southeast Asian country with rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, delicious cuisine, and warm, friendly people, making it a popular destination for travelers worldwide.

City Destinations

Bangkok

  • Bustling cityscape and vibrant nightlife
  • Ornate temples and cultural landmarks
  • Exquisite street food scene
  • Floating markets and river life

City of Angels

Bangkok, the vibrant capital of Thailand, is a bustling metropolis known for its rich cultural heritage, bustling markets, ornate temples, and vibrant street life, creating a captivating fusion of tradition and modernity.

Phuket

  • Beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters
  • Vibrant nightlife and entertainment
  • Rich cultural heritage and temples
  • Exciting water sports activities

Pearl of the Andaman

Phuket, a popular island destination in Thailand, boasts stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and a rich cultural heritage. It offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and entertainment for visitors from around the world.

Pattaya

  • Beach resort city
  • Nightlife and entertainment hub
  • Vibrant street markets
  • Stunning coastal views

Sin City

Pattaya is a vibrant coastal city in Thailand known for its bustling nightlife, beautiful beaches, and entertainment options. It attracts tourists with its lively atmosphere, water sports, shopping, and a wide range of dining experiences.
